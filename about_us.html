ABOUT US

History

Lunarch Apparel was a meer hobby that turned into an intense passion. Lunarch Apparel is a small business that was created by Tania Parker in 2024. She first engaged with shoe making as she was intriged by the thought and decided to join shoe making classes. She then fell in love with the craft and decided to try out making bags. She soon fell deeply passionate about that too and decided to make something out of her newfound skill, thus deciding to create Lunarch Apparel. Tania first started using Moksi as a platform to start selling her products on, however she wanted to expand the brand and chose to create a website for the business! Funny story, the name Lunarch Apparel is a mix of her two dogs names, Luna and Archer, her most loved fur babies. So that is how her love for handmaking these leather goods led her to the creation of her passion project. Tania hopes to further expand the business by making other genuine leather goods such as sunglasses pouches, wallets and belts. Thank you for taking your time to read our short story on how we came to be! Hope you enjoy our website.

Values

Our values are to handmake our products out of genuine leather goods and produce authentic, durable, and quality products that will satisfy the environment and the customers. We also value our customers and want them to have the best experience with us.

Mission

Our mission is be as sustainable as possible while making our products. We want to create high-quality, functional accessories that would last a life time.
